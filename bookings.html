About Online Booking

Online Booking is an appointment booking website used by (mainly) hair salons and beauty spas. It's add-on feature for Kitomba customers, and is the main area where Kitomba's client's clients interact directly with a Kitomba systems. It therefore has a much wider reach than the main Kitomba system used by stylists/receptionists/etc.

How good is it?

  • At launch, bookings up by 25% overnight
  • Handles nearly $2m in bookings every week

What I did

The old booking system stunk, so I yada yada
